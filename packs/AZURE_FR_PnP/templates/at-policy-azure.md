### 4.0 Awareness and Training Controls

#### 4.1 AT-2 Literacy Training and Awareness
Security and privacy literacy training must be provided to system users including managers, senior executives, and contractors:
-	As part of initial training for new users and at least annually thereafter; and
-	When required by system changes or significant updates to literacy training and awareness content and/or lessons learned from incidents that have significant impacts to literacy training and awareness content. 

The following techniques to increase the security and privacy awareness of system users must be employed: at least the internal security alerts, advisories, and directives as deemed necessary, in accordance with the SI-5 control. 

Literacy training and awareness content must be updated at least annually and following assessment or audit findings, lessons learned from internal or external security incidents or breaches recommendations received from  employees via Feedback Issues, changes in applicable laws, executive orders, directives, regulations, policies, standards, and guidelines. 

Lessons learned from internal or external security incidents or breaches must be incorporated into literacy training and awareness techniques.

#### 4.2 AT-2(2) Insider Threat
Literacy training on recognizing and reporting potential indicators of insider threat must be provided. 

#### 4.3 AT-2(3) Social Engineering and Mining
Literacy training on recognizing and reporting potential and actual instances of social engineering and social mining must be provided. 

#### 4.4 AT-3 Role-Based Requirements
Role-based security and privacy training must be provided to personnel with the following roles and responsibilities: Incident Response and Contingency Planning. The training must be provided under the following circumstances: 
- Before authorizing access to the system, information, or performing assigned duties, and at least annually thereafter; and
- When required by system changes. 

Role-based training content must be updated at least annually and following assessment or audit findings, security incidents or breaches and any subsequent lessons learned, and/or changes in applicable laws, executive orders, directives, regulations, policies, standards, and guidelines.
Lessons learned from internal or external security incidents or breaches must be incorporated into role-based training. 

#### 4.5 AT-4 Security Training Records
Information security and privacy training activities shall be documented and monitored, including security and privacy awareness training and specific role-based security and privacy training.  (AT-4a)
Individual training records shall be retained {{trainingRecordsRetention}} after completion of a specific training program.
